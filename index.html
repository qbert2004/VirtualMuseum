Quote from Minister of Culture Aida Balaeva 2018


"Kazakh national instruments are not only part of our cultural heritage, but also living witnesses to the richness and depth of our history. They carry the spirit of our people, their traditions, customs and wisdom passed on from generation to generation. Preserving and promoting these tools is not just a preservation of the past, but also an investment in the future, because through them we preserve our identity and uniqueness in a world of cultural diversity.


Kazakh national instruments reflect the richness of our musical culture and the depth of our emotions. They are an integral part of our national identity, symbolizing our unity, strength and national pride. Their sounds contain the entire depth of our history and the beauty of our nature. Therefore, caring for Kazakh national instruments is not only respect for the past, but also responsibility for the future of our nation"
